How Do You Become More Valuable?

Creating is the most powerful human ability.

If you think, act, and see the world exactly like everyone else, you will produce what everyone else produces. You may fit in, but you will contribute little that is uniquely yours.

This does not mean you should reject normality. Normality creates connection. Without common ground like shared language, communication breaks down.

What usually gives something value is a mix of familiarity and novelty. An idea needs enough familiarity to be understood and enough novelty to be worth considering.

A person who is completely normal becomes replaceable. A person who is completely strange becomes disconnected. Neither creates much value.

Stand between conformity and eccentricity. Share enough with others to be understood and differ enough from others to create something new.

What is something only you can create?

No one has your exact experiences, perspective, interests, skills, and observations. Build on this difference. Create work, ideas, values, and ways of living that carry your fingerprint.
